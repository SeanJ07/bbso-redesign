Before You Visit

What to Know

Physical Requirements

Visitors must walk 1,000 feet on a gravel road and navigate three flights of stairs to access the telescope. Not recommended for children under 5.

Finding the Observatory

Half a mile west of the Big Bear Discovery Center on Hwy 38 (North Shore Drive). Private property; visits are by guided tour only.

Donations Welcome

Tours are free, but donations are strongly encouraged to support public outreach and docent programs.

Can't Make It?

Watch the Sun in near real-time through our live webcam, or explore virtual tours.
