What to Know

Physical Requirements

Visitors must walk 1,000 feet on a gravel road and navigate three flights of stairs to access the telescope. Not recommended for children under 5.

Finding the Observatory

Half a mile west of the Big Bear Discovery Center on Hwy 38 (North Shore Drive). Private property; visits by guided tour only.

Donations Welcome

Tours are free, but donations are strongly encouraged to support outreach and docent programs.
