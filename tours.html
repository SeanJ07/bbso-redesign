BBSO Tours

Starting May 28 2026 and until Sept 3 2026, the Big Bear Solar Observatory will be offering tours in a limited capacity. Tours will be on May 28, June 11, July 9, July 23, Aug 6, Aug 20, and Sept 3. Please note that the tour is not recommended for children under age of 5.

Tours will start at 2pm and last for one hour. Tours are FREE but donations are strongly encouraged.

All 2026 tours are fully booked. There might be cancelations, please check this online form regularly, or contact the office at 909-866-5791x210, or email at .

Feel free to check out some of our virtual tours.

Big Bear Solar Observatory is owned and operated by the New Jersey Institute of Technology, since 1997, and as such it is private property. We have partnered with the Big Bear Valley Astronomical Society to provide docent tour guides and have now begun scheduling tours for the public.

Visiting

The observatory is 1/2 mile west of the Discovery Center on Hwy 38/North Shore Drive. For more detailed directions click here.

Please understand that due to the limitations of our docent volunteers and the physical size of the observatory, tours must remain small and limited in size. All attending must be able to walk 1000' down and back on a gravel road as well as up and down three flights of stairs to access the telescope.

Acknowledgements: BBSO operation is supported by US NSF AGS-2309939 grant and New Jersey Institute of Technology. GST operation is partly supported by the Korea Astronomy and Space Science Institute and the Seoul National University.
