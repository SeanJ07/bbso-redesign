Before You Visit

What to Know

Accessibility

Physical Requirements

Visitors must be able to walk 1,000 feet down and back on a gravel road, plus navigate three flights of stairs to access the telescope. The tour is not recommended for children under 5.

Location

Finding the Observatory

The observatory is located half a mile west of the Big Bear Discovery Center on Hwy 38 (North Shore Drive). BBSO is private property; visits are by guided tour only.

Donations

Free, Donations Welcome

Tours are free of charge, but donations are strongly encouraged to help support public outreach and docent programs.

Virtual

Can't Make It?

Check out our virtual tours for an online look inside the observatory. You can also watch the Sun in near real-time through our live webcam.

Get in Touch
