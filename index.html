Research

Advancing Solar Science

From solar flares to coronal rain, BBSO researchers study the dynamic Sun with unprecedented resolution.

Solar Flares & CMEs

Studying the origins and evolution of solar flares and coronal mass ejections that impact space weather.

GST Instrumentation

The 1.6m Goode Solar Telescope provides diffraction-limited observations of the solar surface.

Coronal Rain

Observing condensation and precipitation in the million-degree solar corona.

Live Solar Observations

Watch the Sun in near real-time through BBSO's full-disk H-alpha telescope and other instruments.
