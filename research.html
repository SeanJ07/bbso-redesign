Instrumentation Projects
Goode Solar Telescope (GST)

The Goode Solar Telescope (GST) is a 1.6-meter clear-aperture off-axis Gregorian solar telescope, one of the largest in the world. It provides diffraction-limited observations with adaptive optics.

Science Projects

Intermittency
Study of intermittency in solar convection and turbulent magnetic fields using high-resolution GST observations.

Oscillations
Helioseismology and magnetohydrodynamic waves propagating through the solar interior and atmosphere.

Space Weather
Solar flares, coronal mass ejections, and their impact on Earth's space environment and technology.

Full Disk Hα Observations
Continuous full-disk monitoring in H-alpha, capturing flares, filaments, and prominences in real time.

Earthshine
Measuring sunlight reflected from Earth to the Moon, providing data on Earth's reflectance and climate monitoring.
