Instrumentation

The Goode Solar Telescope

The GST is a 1.6-meter clear-aperture off-axis Gregorian system with active and adaptive optics. It is one of the world's largest solar telescopes, capable of resolving features as small as 50 km on the Sun's surface.

Equipped with a suite of post-focus instruments including the Near-Infrared Imaging Spectropolarimeter (NIRIS), the Broadband Filter Imager (BFI), and the Fast Imaging Solar Spectrograph (FISS), the GST provides multi-wavelength observations at diffraction-limited resolution.
