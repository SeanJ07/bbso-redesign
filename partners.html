Partners

BBSO collaborates with leading solar physics institutions and observatories worldwide.

Partner Organizations

GONG — Global Oscillation Network Group
A network of six telescopes around the world that observe the Sun continuously, providing helioseismology data.

SOLIS — Synoptic Optical Long-term Investigations of the Sun
A suite of three instruments that provide long-term synoptic observations of solar magnetic activity. Now installed at BBSO.

Hinode
A Japanese solar observation satellite carrying three advanced telescopes for studying the Sun's magnetic field.

SDO/HMI — Solar Dynamics Observatory / Helioseismic and Magnetic Imager
NASA's SDO mission provides continuous observations of the Sun, and HMI measures solar magnetic fields and velocities.

KASI — Korea Astronomy and Space Science Institute
A key international partner supporting GST operations and instrument development.
